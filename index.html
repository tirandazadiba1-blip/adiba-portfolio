A little about myself

I'm a Senior Frontend Developer based in Pune, India, with over six years of experience building enterprise-grade web applications that scale. My core expertise is in Angular 16+ and React, with deep knowledge of TypeScript, RxJS, and state management patterns.

I've worn many hats — developer, Scrum Master, tech lead, and client communicator. I've led teams, managed stakeholder relationships, conducted code reviews, and deployed to production across multiple environments. I don't just write code; I own outcomes.
